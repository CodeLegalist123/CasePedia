Fatal Family Dispute Over Mistress

According to the prosecution case, the accused wanted to keep Kamlibai, the widow of his brother Vasant, as his mistress, but she refused to be his mistress., On the day of the incident the appellant was forcibly trying to drag Kamlibai to his house., At that very time, he was told by his brother Mahu (the deceased) that he could not force and drag Kamlibai to his house., At this intervention, the accused got enraged by such unwarranted advice and therefore lifted a wooden plank (pat) and hit Mahu with it on his head, and also kicked and punched him., Mahu died on the spot., Complaint of this assault was lodged by one Yeshubai who is closely related to both the accused and the victim., On receipt of this report the investigation was conducted and the accused was arrested.
