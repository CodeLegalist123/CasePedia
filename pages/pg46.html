Murder of P2

The brief facts of the case, as per the prosecution story, are that on 5.1.2001 at about 6:10 p.m. at L1, the accused persons formed an unlawful assembly and in prosecution of the common object of such assembly, committed the murder of P2. P3, son of the deceased (PW-3) lodged an Ejahar about the incident at Police Station on 5.1.2001 at about 10:00 p.m., On receipt of the Ejahar, F.I.R. No.3 was registered by Police Station and started investigation., The police arrived at the place of occurrence and called the Executive Magistrate who prepared the inquest on the dead body and the inquest was sent for post-mortem examination to Civil Hospital., The police found one bag containing one dagger and two hand-made bombs lying near the dead body.
